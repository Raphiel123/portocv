The Unknown Shadows
By Rafi

Sinopsis

Rei, a university photography student, discovers an abandoned house on the edge of town rumored to be haunted. While exploring it to capture atmospheric photos for an assignment, Rei finds an old photograph of a little girl named Lila, staring straight into the camera with unsettling realism.

As Rei ventures deeper into the house, strange things begin to happen the door locks on its own, footsteps echo behind him, and the photos he takes start to change. One terrifying truth becomes clearer with every step

The person responsible for Lila’s disappearance is not a ghost. He’s still alive… and hiding somewhere inside the house with Rei.
